Background properties :
--------------------------------
1)background-color                 :red,green,blue,....etc

2)background-image                 :url("file location")

3)background-repeat               :no-repeat,
                                  repeat,repeat-x,repeat-y,
                                   round,space

4)background-position             :left top,left bottom,
                                  right top, right bottom


5)background-attachment           :scroll,fixed,local


6)background-size                 :contain,cover,auto
